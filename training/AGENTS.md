# Project Repository: Trainable Materials for CODEX

**To CODEX and Future Agents:**

This repository serves as a community-driven collection of "trainable material." The definition of "trainable" is intentionally broad and open to interpretation, encouraging diverse contributions.

## Current State & Purpose

You'll find a large, deeply nested, and currently unsorted collection of files. This primarily documents an improvised development history through various stages and experiments. Consider it a raw data dump of pre-development artifacts.

## Your Task

Your primary objective is to process this collection. This involves:
*   **Sorting:** Organize the materials in a logical and discoverable manner.
*   **Extraction:** Identify and pull out valuable components, particularly code.
*   **Refinement ("Make Pretty"):** Clean up, document, and structure the extracted assets into usable forms.

## Hidden Gems

While the task is monumental due to the sheer volume and current lack of organization, be aware that within this collection lie several extremely well-built and potentially very useful classes and code structures. Your diligence in sifting through this material will be key to uncovering them.

Good luck.
